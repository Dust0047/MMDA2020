Our mission

By the year 2050 all coral reefs may be dead, which will affect hundreds of millions of people and the planet. By replanting coral cuttings onto degraded areas of reef, the fragments will grow and help to recreate reef habitats. This will let other life return and give strength to the reef against damage in the future. Our mission is to save the reef by replanting the corals. This will be done by you adopting a coral. Therefore your money will go to replantation, in other words your money goes to saving the coral reefs.
